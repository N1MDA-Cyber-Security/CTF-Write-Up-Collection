The flag is divided into 5 parts :
1. Social media ID 
2. The name of university she studied at
just the abbreviation is enough, for example Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember become ITS
3. The mascot name
4. The time when she uploaded photos in the bookstore
5. The comment in her photo with Sakura

Format as below :
ARA2023{SocialMediaID_UniversityName_MascotName_DateMonthYear-Hour:Minute_RedactedFlag}
Example :
ARA2023{46152324397_UTL_Felda_7Mei2017-13:02_r3d4cTED}
